A 20-year-old graduate from the University of Ilorin voted the Most Influential Student of the Year, First Time Senator of the year and Best Student Personality of the year (all merit awards) by student associations as well as the recipient of the Dotun Ajegbile Foundation Scholarship and Aliu Abdulkabir Foundation Scholarship. He is also an award winning essayist with a growing experience in broadcast media having volunteered as an On-Air Radio Presenter at Unilorin FM 89.3FM. He led the digital campaign and strategy for The Ambassadors Summit 5.0. In the first week alone, registrations scaled with over 150 registrations recorded resulting in over 1500 registrations eventually and increased engagement on all social media and marketing platforms. He has also worked with the Emerging Media team to deliver the Nigerian Innovation Summit 2016 and 2017, West African Regional Conference 2017 and 2018, African Clean up Initiative 7th Year anniversary amongst several others. He currently leads the Digital Marketing team of Mahogany International, an Events Productions and management company with feats across Africa and beyond. Before now, Abdulsamod has worked with Utiva, an Edtech start-up leveraging technology to equip young people with skills to thrive in the job market, and Kudimoney, a start-up deploying technology solutions to help people save conveniently, faster and on-the-go.
