ABOUT VGDI

Vibrant Girls Development Initiative is a youth-led non-for-profit organisation that is commited to the Development of girls especially those under the age of 20. VGDI seeks to help young girls channel their energies into positive causes and groom them into vibrant women in the society. we engange in mentorship sessions,competitions,trainings and seminars,awareness,advocacy, and personal development of girls.

VISION

To see young girls grow into vibrant women in the society.

MISSION

To reduce the prevalent rate of teenage pregnancy,pre-mature sexual activities and gender based violence among girls while ensuring their development.

TARGET AUDIENCE
